Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-19

The Price → Impact Chrome extension does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data. Everything runs locally in your browser.

What the extension does

  • Reads prices visible on the page when you visit a supported shopping site (Amazon.com / .ca / .com.au / .co.uk / .de / .fr / .it / .es / .nl).
  • Renders a small badge next to each price showing the equivalent impact of that amount donated to a high-impact charity.
  • Stores your preferences (selected charity, paused state, per-site disable list, round-up jar balance, donation-intent history) in chrome.storage.sync. That blob is private to your Chrome profile and syncs across devices you're signed into.

What the extension does NOT do

  • No analytics, no telemetry, no error reporting service.
  • No tracking pixels, no fingerprinting, no third-party requests.
  • No data leaves your browser. The extension makes zero network calls.
  • No reading of pages outside the host_permissions declared in the manifest.

Donation links

Each impact badge is a hyperlink. Clicking it opens the relevant charity's donation page (Every.org or the charity's own donate page) in a new tab. From that point on you are on the charity's own site; their privacy practices apply, not this extension's.

Donation history

A click on a badge appends a local entry (timestamp, USD amount, charity id, source hostname). This list lives entirely in chrome.storage.sync and is shown in the extension's options page. It's labelled "Intended donations" because we cannot verify completion across origins. You can clear the history at any time from the options page.
